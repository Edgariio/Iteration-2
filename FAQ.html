Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



1. How do I submit a report?

To submit a report, go to the main page and click on "Report a Problem", enter the necessary information and submit.


2. How do I edit a report?

To edit a report, go to the main page and click on "Edit Problem", modify any information you wish and re-submit the report.


3. How do I cast a vote on existing reports?

To vote on a reported problem go to the main page and click "Vote". Scroll through the list of reported problems and vote for the ones you believe are important.


4. How long does it take for a problem to be resolved?

This usually depends on the type of problem reported. In most cases, problems can be resolve in 4-5 business days.
